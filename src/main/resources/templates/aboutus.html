Mission

We think that looking at plants make us happier, especially during the start of the COVID pandemic when we had to work from home. Being in a space while working continuously can feel dreary. Having a plant is like having a good friend around, who also supplies you with fresh air!

Vision

We want everyone to enjoy and appreciate natural beauty, even in imperfections. You will start looking forward and anticipate when your plant starts growing and when it starts unflurling new leaves to show you how happy it is.
